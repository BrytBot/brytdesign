My Story

From a spark of inspiration to a burning passion.

My name is Bright Quansah, a passionate young graphic designer and the creative mind behind Bryt Design. My design journey began at the age of 13, sparked by a simple moment of curiosity. I was using my uncle’s laptop mostly to play games until one day I stumbled upon an Adobe Photoshop course. That unexpected discovery opened the door to a whole new creative world. I began teaching myself, exploring design with passion and consistency. When the laptop was no longer available, I found Canva and continued learning. From there, I explored many other tools, each one sharpening my skills. Though I had no formal teacher at the time, I stayed committed — and that’s how Bryt Design was born.

Today, Bryt Design is built on the mission to provide clean, impactful, and globally inspired design solutions. My vision is to help brands tell their stories visually, creating connections that transcend borders.
